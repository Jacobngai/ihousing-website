## Why This Page

The About page is a critical trust-building page for:
- Brand searches ("ihousing melaka", "ihousing airbnb")
- Due diligence by potential customers evaluating the company
- Credibility validation before contacting

Having inconsistent authority signals (100+ vs 700+) creates cognitive dissonance and potentially hurts trust. FAQ schema enables rich snippets for company-related queries.

## Expected Impact

- **Trust Building:** Consistent 700+ authority signals across all pages
- **Rich Snippets:** FAQ schema for company-related queries
- **Brand Search CTR:** Improved title with year and authority signal
- **Conversions:** FAQ addresses common evaluation questions upfront
