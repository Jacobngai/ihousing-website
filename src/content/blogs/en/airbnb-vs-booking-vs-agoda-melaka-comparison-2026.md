## 1. Airbnb: The Lifestyle King

Airbnb remains the household name. In Melaka, it is the dominant platform for "staycations" and weekend trips from Kuala Lumpur and Singapore.

**The Melaka Guest Profile:**
- Young couples and families
- Seeking "Instagrammable" interiors
- Weekend-heavy bookings (Friday/Saturday)
- Heavy focus on reviews and "Superhost" status

**Pros for Melaka Hosts:**
- **Host Protection:** AirCover provides solid peace of mind for property damage.
- **Guest Quality:** The review system allows us to screen out "party guests" before they check in.
- **Easy Communication:** The app makes it simple to coordinate check-ins at places like Silverscape or Atlantis.

**Cons:**
- **Fierce Competition:** Every new host starts on Airbnb first.
- **Algorithm Sensitivity:** One bad review can tank your visibility for weeks.

---

## 2. Booking.com: The Mid-Week Savior

If Airbnb is for "fun," Booking.com is for "travel." While many hosts ignore this platform because it "feels like a hotel site," that is exactly why you need to be on it.

**The Melaka Guest Profile:**
- International travelers (Europe, US, Australia)
- Last-minute bookers
- Business travelers visiting Melaka Raya or Ayer Keroh
- Older travelers who prefer traditional "Hotel-style" reliability

**Pros for Melaka Hosts:**
- **Weekday Bookings:** International travelers don't just stay on weekends. They book 3-4 nights starting on a Tuesday, filling your "dead zones."
- **Massive Reach:** Booking.com has a much larger global marketing budget than Airbnb.
- **Higher Intent:** Guests on Booking.com are often further along in their travel planning and less likely to "window shop."

**Cons:**
- **No Guest Reviews (Initially):** You can't see guest reviews before they stay, making screening harder.
- **Cancellation Rates:** Because many bookings are "Pay at Property," cancellation rates can be higher if not managed correctly.

---

## 3. Agoda Homes: The Asia Specialist

You cannot talk about Melaka tourism without talking about Agoda. Since Melaka relies heavily on Singaporean and domestic Malaysian tourists, Agoda is a powerhouse.

**The Melaka Guest Profile:**
- Singaporean families on long weekends
- Domestic Malaysian travelers
- Budget-conscious travelers looking for "Value for Money"

**Pros for Melaka Hosts:**
- **Singapore Market:** Agoda is often the #1 choice for Singaporeans booking trips across the border.
- **Agoda VIP Program:** Listing your home as an "Agoda Home" gives you access to their loyalty program members who book more frequently.
- **Bundle Deals:** Agoda often bundles stays with flights or buses, driving traffic you’d never find on Airbnb.

**Cons:**
- **Lower Price Point:** Agoda guests are often very price-sensitive, which can lead to "price wars" if you aren't careful.
- **Complex Dashboard:** Managing Agoda settings can be a headache for DIY hosts.
